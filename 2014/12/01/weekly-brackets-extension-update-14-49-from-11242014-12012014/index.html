Weekly Brackets Extension Update - 14/49

Filed Under Update

Welcome to This Week’s Brackets Extension Updates, Issue 14/49.

Added or updated @Brackets in the week from 11/24/2014 to 12/01/2014.

This week there were 6 new Extensions and 18 updates. This includes themes and other extensions.

What’s going on

The Brackets team is eagerly working on the 1.1 Release. We are currently reviewing a lot of open pull requests to get as many into the new release as possible. We are also working on getting CEF version 2171 integrated into Brackets. This is a huge step forward for Brackets. The current CEF version uses Chrome 29, whereas the new CEF version will give us Chrome 39! This means bugfixes, a lot of improvements and especially new DevTools.

So, we will have some exciting times ahead of us before we call out the new release. In the meantime, pleae stay tuned and get the latest Brackets build and let us know what you think.
Thanks!
