Weekly Brackets Extension Update - 14/40

Filed Under Update

Welcome to This Week’s Brackets Extension Updates, Issue 14/40.

Added or updated @Brackets in the week from 09/22/2014 to 09/29/2014.

This week there were 6 new Extension and 18 updates. This includes themes and other extensions.

What’s going on?

The Brackets team is currently wrapping up Release 0.44. If you are interested to test Brackets before it will be released in the next couple of days, then this is the right time to jump in. Read more here. We are happy to hear your feedback.

If you don’t have the latest Brackets Release 0.43, get it from brackets.io. Let the team know what you think.
