Weekly Brackets Extension Update - 14/45

Filed Under Update

Welcome to This Week’s Brackets Extension Updates, Issue 14/45.

Added or updated @Brackets in the week from 10/27/2014 to 11/03/2014.

This week there were 6 new Extensions and 16 updates. This includes themes and other extensions.

What’s going on?

This time live from New York. The Brackets team announced release 1.0 of Brackets today at the Future of Web Design Conference.

This time Brackets ships with the Extract extension, that will help to bridge the gap between developer and designer workflows.
You can download Brackets with the Extract extension from Brackets.io.

Give it a try and let us know what you think of it.

NOTE: You need a free Adobe Creative Cloud Account to use the Extract extension.
