Weekly Brackets Extension Update - 14/25

Filed Under Update

Welcome to This Week’s Brackets Extension Updates, Issue 14/25.
@Brackets Extensions who have been added or updated in the week from 06/09/2014 to 06/15/2014.

This week there were 2 new Extensions and 17 updates.

A lot updates to already great extensions. Check out the new extension Autosave on View Change. This extension will automatically save your current document when you switch to another document or when the Brackets window loses it’s focus.

Some view stats from twitter:

  • White Space Sanitizer has been viewed 81 times
  • CodeOverview has been viewed 66 times

As usual, all extensions are available from the Brackets Extension Manager. Or you can download the extension via the link provided in this post.

Want more? Check out Brackets Weekly Episode 3.
