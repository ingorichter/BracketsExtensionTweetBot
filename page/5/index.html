Weekly Brackets Extension Update - 14/49

Filed Under Update

Welcome to This Week’s Brackets Extension Updates, Issue 14/49.

Added or updated @Brackets in the week from 11/24/2014 to 12/01/2014.

This week there were 6 new Extensions and 18 updates. This includes themes and other extensions.

What’s going on

The Brackets team is eagerly working on the 1.1 Release. We are currently reviewing a lot of open pull requests to get as many into the new release as possible. We are also working on getting CEF version 2171 integrated into Brackets. This is a huge step forward for Brackets. The current CEF version uses Chrome 29, whereas the new CEF version will give us Chrome 39! This means bugfixes, a lot of improvements and especially new DevTools.

So, we will have some exciting times ahead of us before we call out the new release. In the meantime, pleae stay tuned and get the latest Brackets build and let us know what you think.
Thanks!

This week there were 10 new Extensions and 31 updates. This includes themes and other extensions.

What’s going on?

We made it! We’ve declared Brackets 1.0 last Tuesday in New York during the Future of Web Design Conference. The response was overwhelming and we got a huge spike in Brackets downloads. Brackets 1.0 comes pre-configured with Extract for Brackets (Preview). This extension will help designers and developers alike to access and extract information from a PSD file. Upload a PSD file to the Creative Cloud and try this great feature.

NOTE: You need a free Adobe Creative Cloud Account to use the Extract extension.

The Extract Extension is available from the right toolbar and from the Edit Menu in Brackets. If you have to extract data from PSD, give it a try and let us know what you think of it. We like to hear the good and the bad. Otherwise we can’t improve. ;-)
