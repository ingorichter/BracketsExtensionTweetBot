Weekly Brackets Extension Update - 15/07

Filed Under Update

Welcome to This Week’s Brackets Extension Updates, Issue 15/07.

Added or updated @Brackets extensions in the week from 02/02/2015 to 02/09/2015.

Last week there were 2 new Extensions and 27 updates. This includes themes and other extensions.

What’s going on?

We are still working on the the upcoming Brackets 1.2 release. There are still a couple of PR’s under review and a couple of issues needs to be resolved. Stay tuned!

Have a great week!
