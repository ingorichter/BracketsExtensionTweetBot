Weekly Brackets Extension Update - 15/06

Filed Under Update

Welcome to This Week’s Brackets Extension Updates, Issue 15/06.

Added or updated @Brackets extensions in the week from 01/26/2015 to 02/02/2015.

Last week there were 3 new Extensions and 17 updates. This includes themes and other extensions.

We are currently getting ready to release Brackets 1.2 in the upcoming weeks. If you need something fixed for this release, then this is a good time to contribute. Bugfixes, translations and documentation. We welcome every kind of contribution.

Have a great week!

In the meantime, you can find us on these social networks

  • Facebook
  • Google Plus
  • twitter

Share your thoughts and stories, ask questions and get in touch with the team.

As usual, you can download the latest Brackets release. Please let us know what you think.
